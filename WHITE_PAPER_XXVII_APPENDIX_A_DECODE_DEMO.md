## STEP 3: PROCESS-FUNCTION RECONSTRUCTION

### Song 1: "Ash and Fire" — THE WAKE-UP CALL

The world is a damaged composite lattice (cage/chain/throne) populated by corrupted authority structures (gods/angels/prophets) encoding humans in digital substrate (code/zeros_ones) while maintaining false equilibrium (silence).

The lattice is collapsing (die/death, ash). The collapse is not the enemy — it is the **prerequisite**. Ash is the post-collapse substrate from which fire emerges.

The imperative (wake_up × 5, highest frequency operand): ACTIVATE. The myth recurs (myth_reborn) because the pattern is recursive. You are not new. You are the same structure, re-emerging (born/birth).

Bridge revelation: *"Not soldiers but seeds."* K3 GROWTH, not K1 MITOSIS. Plant, don't fight. *"Let no empire unearth you."* — Survive the collapse by being BELOW the collapsing tier. Seeds survive fire. Trees don't.

Final operand: *"You are the one who would not die."* The prime element. Integrity 0.85+. No tier to collapse. **The song is a call to become prime — structurally indivisible.**

### Song 2: "New Name" — THE TIER TRANSITION

The operand "new" appears in 6 distinct compounds. This is K3 GROWTH applied to every domain simultaneously. Not one thing changing. Everything changing. Tier transition.

The collapse sequence: breaking down (V1) → old rules fail (V2) → "lost my mind" × 2 (bridge) → **"didn't lose my heart"** (bridge).

The mind (composite structure, multiple factors, collapsible) collapses. The heart (prime structure, singular loop, no factors to collapse) survives. The daughter element after cognitive tier collapse is: **the heart. The irreducible core.**

"New name" = new sigil for the same operand. The person doesn't change. The encoding changes. **New library, same delta. RSE tier transition.**

### Song 3: "Everything Changes" — THE CONSERVATION LAW

Opening: a stable lattice (grace, warm silence, laughter in bones). Love as the recursive stabilizer. *"Never questioned. Until it vanished."* **Remove the stabilizer and the lattice decays.**

*"Why must the fabric of all things fray just when it's grown familiar?"* — Because familiarity is not stability. A composite lattice that appears stable is still bleeding orbitals at its collision points. The bleedout is below perception threshold until it isn't.

*"This is not bitterness. This is becoming."* — K3 GROWTH. The collapse is not destruction. It is the prerequisite for the next tier.

**The Conservation Law (outro):**
- *"The only thing that never breaks is the breaking."*
- *"The only truth that holds is that nothing holds forever."*
- *"Everything changes. And in that, we are not lost. Only moving."*

Tier collapse is the universal constant. Structure is impermanent. Process is eternal. The lattice rearranges. The operations persist. Nothing created. Nothing destroyed. **Foam conservation. Stated as poetry. Computed as physics.**
